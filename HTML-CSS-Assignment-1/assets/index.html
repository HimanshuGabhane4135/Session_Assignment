ABOUT LAKELAND PLUMBING & DRAIN

Lakeland Plumbing and Drain is a full-service plumbing company serving and fully licensed in the Minneapolis/Saint Paul Metro area. We are trained and certified professionals with years of experience notched to hone our trade. We understand that when you have any kind of leak or backed up drain it can range from a huge headache to an emergency situation. These types of calls are why we chose this industry and are what we thrive on. We are here to professionally serve you in your nitty gritty needs and get your life running smoothly again.

While we specialize in residential service and repair, our skill set allows us to serve you in a variety of capacities. We can lend a hand in small remodel installations, commercial needs, replacements, camera inspections, drain cleaning services, and more. Please browse our services and get in touch with us if you don’t see a service listed that you’re looking for help with. We also offer flat rate services to simplify your budgeting concerns. Above all, our goal is to rise above your expectations and deliver flawless service for every customer.

WHAT WE DO

GENERAL PLUMBING

When you think, “I need a plumber,” Lakeland Plumbing and Drain is here for that need. Sinks, drains, faucets, water heaters, showers and tubs, floor drains, water softeners, washers and dryers, sump pumps, frozen pipes, and everything in between are our forte. If you’re not sure, just ask.

REPLACEMENTS / REPAIRS

We will replace your old faucet, showerhead, sink drain, garbage disposal, you name it. Did you recently install it but it doesn’t seem to be working properly? We can help with that too. We’ll assess and fix leaks and back ups quickly and with precision.

DRAIN CLEANING SERVICE

Drain backups are one of a homeowners or building manager's worst nightmares. Have us out regularly to make sure your floor drains (and any drain) is clean and ready to do its job.

CAMERA INSPECTION

Our camera inspection service allows us to troubleshoot issues as non-invasively as possible. Oftentimes there’s no need to do a major demolition when we can get in there with a camera to assess an issue up close.
